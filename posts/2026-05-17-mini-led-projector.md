## Key Considerations

- **Resolution matters** — Some cheap projectors have poor resolution, leading to returns and bad reviews. Promote models with at least 1080p support (even if native is lower) and good brightness ratings (200+ ANSI lumens).
- **Audio is a differentiator** — Built-in speakers on budget projectors are notoriously bad. Suggest Bluetooth speaker pairings as an upsell opportunity.
- **Compare, don't oversell** — Honest comparisons with TV alternatives build trust and reduce return rates.
