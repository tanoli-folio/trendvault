Cookie Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how TrendVault uses them, and how you can control them. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember things about your visit — such as your preferred theme, whether you've consented to analytics — and allow advertisers and analytics providers to recognise you across sessions.

2. Categories of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These cookies are essential for the Site to function. They include cookies that remember your cookie consent choice and your theme preference. They cannot be switched off.

Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics (and may use Google Analytics) to understand how visitors find and use the Site. These cookies collect aggregated, anonymised information such as which pages are most visited. We only set these cookies if you consent.

Advertising — Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertising on the Site. Google and its partners use cookies (such as the NID, IDE and DSID cookies) to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this Site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Affiliate tracking

When you click an affiliate link on the Site, the destination merchant (e.g. Amazon) may set cookies to track that the click came from TrendVault. We do not control these cookies; refer to the destination site's cookie policy for details.

3. How to control cookies

  • Cookie banner: on first visit you will see a consent banner allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.
  • Browser settings: all major browsers let you view, delete and block cookies. See aboutcookies.org for step-by-step instructions.
  • Opt-out tools: visit Your Online Choices (EU/UK) or the DAA opt-out (US) to opt out of personalised advertising.

4. GDPR cookie consent

If you are visiting from the UK or EEA we will only set non-essential cookies (analytics, advertising) after you have given consent through our cookie banner, in line with the UK GDPR, EU GDPR and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR). You can withdraw consent at any time by clicking the "Cookie settings" link in the footer or by clearing cookies in your browser.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.

6. Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, email privacy@trendvault.pages.dev.
